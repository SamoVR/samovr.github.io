Interactive Elements
Enable or disable optional interactive sections on your portfolio.
✨ Cursor Constellation
Animated constellation of floating dots that connect to each other and react to the visitor's cursor with trails and a glow. Rendered on a canvas behind the whole page.
🔽 Scroll-Reveal Animations
Cards, skill bars, timeline entries, and section headings fade and slide up as you scroll. Skill bars also animate from 0% to their real level on scroll-in.
⚡ Hero Reveal Animation
A short cinematic intro plays once per visit (your name appears centred, holds, fades out) - then your name, bio, buttons, and stats all slide up into place together as one animation. Turning this off shows the hero instantly with no intro and no slide-in.
🖼 Card Image Preview on Hover
When hovering a project card, the first screenshot fades in as a subtle background wash - makes the grid feel alive before clicking anything.
📏 Reading Progress Bar
A thin accent-coloured bar at the top of the page fills as you scroll down. Gives visitors a sense of where they are on the page.
🎮 Secret Button
A tiny invisible button hidden in the corner of the page. Hover over the bottom-right corner to reveal it. Clicking it triggers a full-page glitch effect.
🟢 Status Badge
A small pulsing-dot badge in the hero showing what you're up to right now - a quick signal that you're active.
Shown as-is next to the green dot. Doesn't need to be a specific project - "Currently scripting" or "Currently tinkering" works fine if you just want to show you're active.
🔒 Site Lockdown
Puts a full-screen lock wall over the portfolio. Enforced in JavaScript - cannot be bypassed by disabling CSS or stylesheets. Visitors see your custom message instead of the site.
Shown to visitors when the site is locked.
⌨ Live Terminal
A typing-animation terminal window that appears as a section on your portfolio. Loops through configurable command/output lines.
Shown in the terminal's fake titlebar.
Commands are typed out character-by-character. Output lines appear instantly below them, like a real shell.
Save Interactive Settings
Saves all toggles - cursor, scroll-reveal, hero reveal, card preview, progress bar, secret button, status badge, lockdown, and terminal.
